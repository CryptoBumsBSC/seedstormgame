🌿 SEED STORM 🌿

COMPLETE GAME GUIDE

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Survive as long as possible while shooting down enemy buds. You play as "Dudley Bud" - a heroic cannabis leaf firing seeds at incoming enemies. Get the highest score to make the leaderboard!

🌿 YOUR CHARACTER: DUDLEY BUD

🌿
Dudley Bud
That's you!

You control Dudley Bud, an iconic 5-point cannabis leaf with cool sunglasses. Move left and right to dodge enemies and hazards while firing seeds upward to destroy incoming threats.

🎮 CONTROLS

Desktop

  • ← → or A D - Move
  • SPACE - Fire seeds
  • ESC - Pause game

Mobile/Touch

  • Left button - Move left
  • Right button - Move right
  • Fire button - Shoot seeds

❤️ LIVES & DAMAGE

  • You start with 3 LIVES
  • When hit, the screen flashes red and you become invincible for 1.5 seconds
  • During invincibility, you flash and cannot take damage
  • Maximum lives is capped at 3
  • GAME OVER when all lives are lost

👾 ENEMIES (Shoot Them!)

Three types of enemy buds descend from the top. Each type has different health and rewards:

INDICA
1 hit = 1 point
SATIVA
2 hits = 2 points
HYBRID
3 hits = 3 points
  • Enemies shoot back at you - dodge their projectiles!
  • Difficulty increases every 15 seconds
  • After 45 seconds, enemies may spawn in formations (V-shape, diagonal, horizontal)

👹 BOSS ENEMY

👹
MEGA BUD BOSS
10 hits to defeat
  • Spawns every 2 MINUTES
  • Moves side to side across the screen
  • Fires spread shot attacks (3 projectiles)
  • Has a health bar displayed above it
  • Defeating the boss grants BUD RAGE!

🔥 BUD RAGE (Boss Reward)

When you defeat a boss, you gain BUD RAGE - a permanent power-up!

  • PERMANENT 25% FASTER FIRE RATE for the rest of the game
  • 10-SECOND SHIELD protection
  • Stacks with other power-ups
  • Displayed in the HUD when active

🔥 COMBO SYSTEM

Chain kills quickly for bonus points!

  • Kill enemies within 1.5 seconds of each other to build combos
  • 2x MULTIPLIER at 3+ combo
  • 3x MULTIPLIER at 5+ combo
  • Combo counter displayed during streaks
  • Screen shakes at 3+ combo kills!
  • Kill streak displayed at 5+ consecutive kills

🏆 SCORING

  • Indica: 1 point (× combo multiplier)
  • Sativa: 2 points (× combo multiplier)
  • Hybrid: 3 points (× combo multiplier)
  • Boss: 10 points + Bud Rage
  • Higher waves = tougher enemies = more points

💡 PRO TIPS

Survival Tips

  • Keep moving - standing still makes you an easy target
  • Clear enemies before they reach the bottom of the screen
  • Watch for hazards starting at 20 seconds
  • Prioritize dodging over shooting when overwhelmed

Scoring Tips

  • Chain kills quickly for combo multipliers
  • Target Hybrid enemies (orange) for maximum points
  • Survive to 4 minutes for maximum firepower
  • Don't miss the boss - it appears every 2 minutes!

Special Object Tips

  • Always grab the Bud Angel for shield protection
  • During Seed Storm, focus on dodging but SHOOT the white-hot seed!
  • If you have a shield, you can safely touch the Skull
  • Shields stack - grab multiple for extended protection
