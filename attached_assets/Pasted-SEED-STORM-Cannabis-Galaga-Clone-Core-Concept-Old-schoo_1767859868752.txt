SEED STORM - Cannabis Galaga Clone
Core Concept: Old-school vertical space shooter where you play as "Dudley Bud" shooting cannabis seeds at incoming enemy buds of different strains.
Game Mechanics
Player:

Cool cannabis bud character (your mascot)
Fires cannabis seeds as bullets
3 lives total
Death = restart from beginning

Difficulty Progression (Every 15 seconds):

0:00-0:15 → 1 hit kills = 1 point
0:15-0:30 → 2 hits kill = 2 points
0:30-0:45 → 3 hits kill = 3 points
0:45-1:00 → 4 hits kill = 4 points
And so on... up to 10 minutes continuous play
Speed increases, more enemies spawn, bullets get faster

Enemies:

Different strain buds (Indica, Sativa, Hybrid)
Different colors and sizes
Shoot seeds back at player
Health increases every 15 seconds

Scoring:

Points = hits needed to kill
High score tracking
Daily + Weekly leaderboards

Economic Model
Entry Fee:

Pay in EVM token (could be ETH, USDC, or custom Base token)
All entry fees go into prize pool

Weekly Payouts (Automated):

1st Place: 30% of week's revenue
2nd Place: 15% of week's revenue
3rd Place: 5% of week's revenue
50% total distribution = House keeps 50%

Leaderboard Reset:

Weekly reset at specific time (e.g., Sunday 12:00 UTC)
Automatic smart contract payout to top 3 wallets
